Task Overview

Large Language Models (LLMs) are increasingly used to rewrite and rephrase scientific content for different audiences and communication goals.

This shared task focuses on reconstructing and retrieving original research paper titles from LLM-generated reformulations.

Style Categories

  • Technical – Methodology-focused titles.
  • Accessible – Reader-friendly titles.
  • Catchy – Creative and engaging titles.

Each original title is associated with 30 reformulations, consisting of 10 variants for each style.
